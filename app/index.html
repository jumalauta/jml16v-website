Jumalauta Sweet Sixteen

2. — 4. 9. 2016

It’s time for another special celebration of Jumalauta! You only turn sixteen once per each base greater than 6. That calls for the party of a lifetime, of course, so join us, put on a complimentary tiara, open a beverage and let your inner teen princess shine at JML SWEET SIXTEEN!

Of course, we will again have compos of the highest quality, to tell who are the true princesses and who are just boring peons. Further details about the compos can be found below. Life’s never boring when you party with us!

Location

We have once again booked the majestic Härkälinna castle in the heart of Finland, Hauho. The place is about 35 km from Hämeenlina and 60 km from Tampere. For more information about the party place, check http://www2.mokkihaku.fi/cabin/info/id/13085

Tickets are 20€ for a regular entry or 40€ if you want a spot in a cabin (cabins will be handled on a first come, first served basis).

Accommodation

We have also booked some cabins right next to the castle, which are available for our guests. Each of the cabins will of course have beds for a handful of people and they are also equipped with fridges, microwave ovens, bathrooms and other amenities. There's also beds for 20 or so people inside the castle in case you just want to crash there. The beds will have mattresses and pillows, but you should bring your own sleeping gear like sleeping bags or bed sheets.

Arriving

The partyplace is located in Hauho, about 35km from Hämeenlinna. The easiest way to get there is by your own car (or hitching a ride from your fellow Jumalauta members).

By car: Drive 2.5km from Hauho on route 12 towards Lahti. Turn left to route 3223 (Sappee, Luopioinen) and drive about 2 km. Turn left on sign "Leppäniemen Hirsihuvilat" and drive Leppäniemen tie about 2 km. When you arrive to a yellow farm house - you're there!

By public transport: You can get to Hämeenlinna by a train or a bus. Please note that last year's celebration was a special occasion and this time we won't be offering any pickups from Hämeenlinna. So if you need a ride, talk to others who are also going.

For GPS: Leppäniementie 196, 14700 Hauho

Compos

General rules

  • If a production has already been released it will be disqualified
  • A production will only be accepted in a compo if at least one of the authors is present at the party place (no remote entries)
  • You can take part in any compo with several different entries. For example, your group can participate in the demo compo with 7 demos, or you can make 3 tracks for the music compo
  • You can enter your entry in multiple compos as long as other rules apply
  • If the production can not be shown with the compo machines the author has to bring a suitable, allowed-by-the-rules machine on which the production can be shown.
  • The entry must be delivered in a ZIP archive, with descriptive filenames, so it’s 100% clear which file to show/play/run
  • You can test your productions on the compo machine before the compo
  • JML16 organizers have the right to publish the compo results, with your name/nick, and all the compo entries
  • Disqualified entries and entries not shown in the compo will not be spread
